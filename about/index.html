Our Objective

To publish relevant and reliable weekly news that informs citizens, amplifies local voices, and supports responsible decision-making in our communities.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Deliver accurate, people-centered reporting through accessible journalism.

Vision: Build an informed and connected community where every reader feels represented.

Short History

EyeWatch began in 2018 as a two-page bulletin circulated by local volunteers. By 2020, it became a weekly publication featuring education, livelihood, health, and civic updates. Today, EyeWatch is a trusted newspaper shared in both print and digital PDF formats, reaching households, schools, and partner organizations.

With each issue, we continue our commitment to fair reporting, practical information, and stories that celebrate community resilience.
